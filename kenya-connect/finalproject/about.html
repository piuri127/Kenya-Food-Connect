About Kenya Connect

Reducing food waste one connection at a time

Our Mission

Kenya Connect aims to reduce food waste in Kenya by connecting vendors with surplus food to consumers who need it. Our platform provides an efficient way to redistribute excess food, benefiting both businesses and communities.

The Problem We're Solving

In Kenya, approximately one-third of all food produced goes to waste, while many people struggle to access affordable, nutritious food. This disconnect represents both an environmental challenge and a social opportunity.

Our Approach

Our digital platform allows food vendors - including restaurants, grocery stores, bakeries, and farms - to list their surplus food at reduced prices or as donations. Consumers can then purchase or collect this food, reducing waste and saving money.
